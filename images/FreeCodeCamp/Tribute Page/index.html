Life of Nicola Tesla

"The present is theirs; the future, for which I really worked, is mine."
  • 1856 Born in the Austrian Empire
  • 1882 Moved to Paris, started work for the Continental Edison Company
  • 1884 Emigrated to the US
  • 1887 Developed an induction motor ran on alternating current
  • 1889 Came up with the Tesla coil
  • 1895 Started of the NIcola Tesla Company
  • 1898 Introduced radio remote control
  • 1906 Demonstrated bladeless turbine
  • 1946 Died at age of 86
